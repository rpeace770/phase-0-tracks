All About Rocko

I want to introduce my cat, Rocko.

I adopted Rocko two years ago in Austin. He was four years old, so they put him in the senior "apartment". There were two older lady cats and he was hiding under a small, white table. The employee told me that the older women were beating up on Rocko. I reached under the table to pet him and he gently bit my finger. I knew he was saying, "Take me home."

Now, he no longer hides. He is happy and comfortable in his new home. His hobbies include:

  • Napping
  • Watching the birds
  • Chasing yarn or feather toys
  • Snugglin'

These are hobbies for most cats, so let me tell you three things that set him apart from other cats.

  1. Without fail, he gets up from napping at 5pm to find me wherever I am. He sits directly in front of me and stares until I get up to feed him. He doesn't meow or paw at me, he just stares.
